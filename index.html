About LIC Tech Term Plans

LIC Tech Term Plans are non-participating, non-linked term assurance plans offered by the Life Insurance Corporation of India. These plans provide financial protection to policyholders and their families in case of an unforeseen event.

Features

  • Flexible premium payment options
  • Choice of policy terms
  • Option to choose between level sum assured or increasing sum assured
  • Option to include accidental death benefit and critical illness cover
  • No medical examination required for policyholders up to a certain age
  • Policy can be renewed up to a certain age

Benefits

  • Death benefit payable to the nominee in case of the unfortunate demise of the policyholder
  • Option to receive the death benefit as a lump sum or in installments
  • Optional accidental death benefit and critical illness cover
  • Tax benefits under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, 1961
  • Tax benefits under Section 10(10D) of the Income Tax Act, 1961

FAQ

What is the minimum age to buy LIC Tech Term Plans?

The minimum age to buy LIC Tech Term Plans is 18 years.

What is the maximum age to buy LIC Tech Term Plans?

The maximum age to buy LIC Tech Term Plans is 65 years.

What is the policy term for LIC Tech Term Plans?

The policy term for LIC Tech Term Plans can range from 10 years to 40 years, depending on the policyholder's choice.

What is the premium payment term for LIC Tech Term Plans?

The premium payment term for LIC Tech Term Plans can range from single premium payment to regular premium payment, depending on the policyholder's choice.
